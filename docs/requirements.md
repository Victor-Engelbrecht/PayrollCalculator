# Payroll Calculator — Requirements

## Product Requirements

- Manage companies
- Manage employees per company
- Custom additions and deductions (flat amounts)
- Single payment provider integration
- Audit trail of payroll runs (who was paid, what amount, when)
- Email payslip delivery

## Technical Requirements

- Single currency (no multi-currency support)
- Multi-country employees supported via per-country statutory rules (minimum wage, etc.)
- Scalable
- Secure

## Out of Scope (for now)

- Multi-currency calculations
- Payroll approval workflows
- Multiple payment provider integrations
- Multiple communication channels (SMS, portal, etc.)
